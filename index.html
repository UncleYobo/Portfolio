About

James Irei

Welcome! I'm the CTO of Dauntless XR, a pioneering startup focused on crafting enterprise XR applications for esteemed US Government entities such as NASA, Department of Defense, and IRS. My expertise of over a decade lies in leveraging Unity to create cross-platform applications, emphasizing optimal performance on resource-constrained hardware like mobile devices and the HoloLens 2.

Throughout my journey, I've been fortunate to work with Fortune 500 companies, including Walmart and the Department of Interior, where I contributed to projects that left a lasting impact on their operations. Additionally, my time with Artificial Inc, a trailblazing life-science lab automation company, allowed me to witness firsthand how technology can accelerate scientific breakthroughs.

At Dauntless XR, we're driven by challenges and motivated to unlock the potential of XR technology across industries. Collaborating with prestigious government organizations has granted me invaluable insights into their unique needs and aspirations.

On this platform, I'm eager to share my experiences, discoveries, and passion for technology with fellow developers, industry partners, and XR enthusiasts. Let's embark on this journey together, shaping the future of XR applications and embracing the boundless possibilities of innovation.

Thank you for joining me, and I look forward to connecting with you in this exciting realm of XR and software development!
