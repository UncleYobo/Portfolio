Worked as the UI/UX designer for a software solutions company for the aviation industry. Secondary duties included content management/creation, graphic design, designing marketing materials, and style guides. Ensured a consistent look and feel across multiple products and multiple platforms while keeping clear and simplistic iconographic styles.
